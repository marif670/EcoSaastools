How to Use

Simple and effective: spray directly onto skin and gently rub into the area. For best results, use daily on tense muscles or before bed to support restful sleep.

  1. Shake well. Spray 4–8 pumps onto the targeted area.
  2. Massage gently until absorbed. Let dry before dressing.
  3. If new to topical magnesium, start with smaller dose and increase gradually.
Caution: For external use only. Do not apply to broken skin. Avoid eye contact. Discontinue if irritation occurs.
